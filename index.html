Who am I?


My educational endeavours started with an interest in web design. This quickly lead me into UX-design, persuasive design, concept development and that sort of thing. I eventually ended up with a master's degree in Information Architecture which culminated in a thesis about semantically structured data and how this can help create better product recommendations. However, after finishing my studies I realised that the UX-market is quite saturated in terms of the specific types of job listings i wanted to accommodate. This made me pick up the books once again in order to dive into web development. I had a foundation in HTML and CSS but knew nothing about the deeper aspects. All i knew what that i wanted to become and all-around frontender. One who would be able to brainstorm, design, develop, test and publish while undergoing tight project management.
